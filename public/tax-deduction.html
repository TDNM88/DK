Tax Credit for Dust War Expenses

Greetings, brave warriors of Uusimaa! Fantastic news from the Dust Killer team: the Ministry of Cleanliness will cover 60% of your cleaning costs! That’s right, you only pay 40% while the Ministry backs you up in our epic battle against dirt and grime.

How It Works:

When you enlist our heroic cleaning services, the Ministry of Cleanliness steps in to cover 60% of the expenses. This means you save big while keeping your home spotless!

Details:

  • Maximum Credit: 3,500 € per person, 7,000 € for couples.
  • Threshold: 100 € per person annually.
  • Eligible Services: Home, holiday apartment cleaning, and cleaning for parents' or grandparents' homes.

Claiming is Easy:

  • Order our cleaning services and join the Dust Killer crusade.

  • Claim your 60% tax credit.
