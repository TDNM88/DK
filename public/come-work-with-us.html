Join Our Team

Be Part of Something Great

At Dust Killer, we are more than just a cleaning service. We are a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about creating clean, healthy, and vibrant environments for our clients. Our mission is to provide top-notch cleaning services that exceed expectations and make a positive impact on the lives of those we serve. We believe that a clean space is a happy space, and we are committed to making that a reality for our clients.

If you are looking for a rewarding career where you can make a difference every day, Dust Killer is the place for you. We offer a supportive and inclusive work environment where you can grow and thrive. Our team members are valued and appreciated for their hard work and dedication, and we provide the training and support needed to succeed.

Why Work With Us?

At Dust Killer, we believe in investing in our team members. We offer competitive pay, flexible scheduling, and opportunities for advancement within the company. Our comprehensive training programs ensure that you have the skills and knowledge needed to excel in your role. We also provide ongoing support and development opportunities to help you grow your career.

In addition to competitive compensation, we offer a range of benefits to support your well-being. These include health insurance, paid time off, and retirement savings plans. We understand the importance of work-life balance, and we strive to create a flexible and supportive work environment that allows you to thrive both personally and professionally.

Our Values

At the heart of Dust Killer are our core values: integrity, excellence, and respect. We are committed to conducting our business with the highest ethical standards and providing exceptional service to our clients. We believe in treating our team members, clients, and the environment with respect and care. Our values guide everything we do and help us create a positive and inclusive work culture.

We strive for excellence in all aspects of our business. From the quality of our cleaning services to the professionalism of our team members, we are dedicated to exceeding expectations. We believe that by upholding our values, we can build a strong and successful company that makes a positive impact on our clients and the community.

Open Positions

We are always looking for dedicated and hardworking individuals to join our team. Whether you have years of experience or are just starting your career, we have a variety of positions available. Some of the roles we are currently hiring for include:

  • Residential Cleaner: Responsible for providing high-quality cleaning services to our residential clients. Duties include dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and general cleaning tasks.
  • Office Cleaner: Maintain a clean and organized office environment for our commercial clients. Duties include cleaning common areas, restrooms, and workspaces.
  • Specialized Cleaner: Handle specialized cleaning tasks such as carpet cleaning, window cleaning, and post-renovation cleaning. Requires attention to detail and the ability to work with specialized equipment.
  • Team Leader: Oversee a team of cleaners, ensuring that all tasks are completed to the highest standards. Responsible for training new team members and maintaining a positive work environment.

If you are passionate about cleaning and take pride in your work, we would love to hear from you. Join us at Dust Killer and be part of a team that values excellence, integrity, and respect.

How to Apply

Ready to join our team? Applying is easy. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Visit our careers page on our website to view the latest job openings.
  2. Choose the position that best fits your skills and interests.
  3. Submit your application online, including your resume and a cover letter explaining why you want to join Dust Killer and how you can contribute to our team.
  4. Our hiring team will review your application and contact you to schedule an interview if your qualifications match our needs.

We look forward to welcoming you to the Dust Killer family. Together, we can make a positive impact and create cleaner, healthier environments for our clients.
